Our misson

We are revolutionizing the way you access and utilize information within your enterpsie files and information. Our private knowledge search engine is designed to eliminate the struggle in finding crucial insights and make your organisation much more efficent and resilient. With our artifical document-to-data approach, we ensure your organisation transitions towards a cognitive search in the hunt of everyday information without the need to change your "Document Management Systems" and Tools.

About our solution

Our focus is on serving organizations who possess vast repositories of documents spread across multiple locations or document management systems (DMS). These documents encompass a wide range of valuable information such as requirements, Quality management- and business processes, instructions, contracts, and other relevant business data. The core objective of our solution extends beyond mere communication with your files. It aims to locate citate, extract, combine and sumerise hidden information from these inactive or even dead files by engaging you in interactive question-and-answer sessions.
